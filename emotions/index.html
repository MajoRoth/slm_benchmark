Emotional Speakers Compression for SLM Acoustic Benchmark. We generate the samples using a voice cloning model (XTTS) by giving prompts from emotional dataset (RAVDESS). speakers are between 1-24 (odd are men, even are women). Emotions are from ['happy', 'sad', 'neutral'], texts generated by chat GPT. Each audio sample is chosen between 5 samples, when choosing the sample which minimizes word error rate with the text prompt.
